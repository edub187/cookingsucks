About This Blog

This is the story of cooking. Cooking with those you love surrounded by those that you also love but that drive you crazy. I'm talking about you kids. In a perfect world, we all have infinite time to cut veggies, sip wine, and cook food. But that's not reality. Reality is that you have to cut veggies, pull a 2 year old off your leg, knock over your glass of wine, and then sign your kid's homework assignment. That's the reality of cooking in my family, and why I created this blog.
